# Appointment Policy

## Overview
This policy outlines the procedures for scheduling, rescheduling, and canceling medical appointments within our healthcare facility.

## Scheduling Appointments

### How to Schedule
- Call the appointment desk at (555) 123-4567
- Use the online patient portal at www.healthcenter.example.com
- Schedule in person at the front desk during business hours
- Schedule via email: appointments@healthcenter.example.com

### Business Hours
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Sunday and holidays: Closed

## Appointment Types
- Routine checkups: 30 minutes
- Specialist consultations: 45 minutes
- Follow-up visits: 20 minutes
- Initial evaluations: 60 minutes

## Preparation Requirements
- Arrive 15 minutes early for new patient appointments
- Bring insurance card and photo ID
- Complete any pre-visit forms from the patient portal
- List current medications
- Note any recent symptoms or concerns

## Rescheduling Appointments

### How to Reschedule
- Call at least 24 hours in advance
- Use the online patient portal
- Send email to appointments@healthcenter.example.com

### Rescheduling Notice
- Rescheduling requests must be made 24 hours before the appointment
- Requests made less than 24 hours may be treated as cancellations
- Rescheduling is subject to availability

## Cancellation Policy

### Cancellation Procedure
- Notify the appointment desk 24 hours in advance
- Cancel via phone, online portal, or email
- Cancellations without 24-hour notice may result in a $50 cancellation fee

### No-Show Policy
- Patients who do not arrive for scheduled appointments without prior notice are considered "no-shows"
- Two no-shows within 12 months may result in dismissal from the practice
- No-show fees of $75 may be charged

## Missed Appointment Protocols

### First Missed Appointment
- Automated reminder call or message within 24 hours
- Review of health status and rescheduling options
- No fee charged

### Second Missed Appointment
- Call from practice manager with discussion of barriers to attendance
- Opportunity to reschedule
- $50 fee may be charged

### Third Missed Appointment
- Notice that patient may be dismissed from practice
- 30-day opportunity to contact office before dismissal

## Special Circumstances

### Emergency Situations
- Patients experiencing medical emergencies should call 911 or go to the emergency department
- Follow-up appointments can be scheduled after emergency care

### Transportation Issues
- Contact the appointment desk to discuss options
- Community transportation services may be available for eligible patients

### Virtual Visits
- Available for follow-up appointments and consultations
- Schedule same way as in-person appointments
- Requires computer or smartphone with camera and microphone

## Patient Rights

- Right to schedule appointments at convenient times
- Right to know appointment wait times
- Right to reschedule for valid reasons
- Right to receive reminder notifications
